# *The Resource Line* Archive (1999-2003)

*The Resource Line* was a monthly(ish) newsletter produced by the Cedar Rapids Gay and Lesbian Resource Center ("The GLRC") in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, from about 1993 until at least 2003. The GLRC distributed paper copies of *The Resource Line* by mail to its subscribers and in literature racks at local businesses. In later years, *The Resource Line* was also available for download on the GLRC's web site (which could be found at www.crglrc.org from 1999 until August 2010, when the domain registration expired).
